About Us

TripsEdge is a data aggregator for domestic ticket inventory that makes this inventory accessible to online payment platforms, banks and Fintech companies for ticket vending to their customers with zero percent markup on flight ticket.

Key Features
  • Domestic Distribution System

  • Revenue Settlement and management System

  • Global Asset Pay Platform
